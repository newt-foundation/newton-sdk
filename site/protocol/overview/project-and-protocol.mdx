### Overview

The global financial system is being reshaped by two powerful trends. Public blockchains are now core financial infrastructure, settling trillions through stablecoins, RWAs, and DeFi—but institutions remain cautious due to the lack of trusted compliance frameworks. At the same time, AI agents are emerging as autonomous market participants, yet their rapid growth has outpaced the guardrails needed to prevent misuse, fraud, or regulatory violations.

Newton Protocol addresses these gaps with a decentralized policy engine that embeds compliance, risk, and business rules directly into transaction execution. By enabling programmable, privacy-preserving enforcement, Newton transforms compliance from a costly afterthought into a foundational layer institutions, regulators, and AI systems can rely on.

To learn more, read the Newton Protocol litepaper [here](https://blog.newt.foundation/the-litepaper/).

Newton Protocol is the first policy protocol built to govern the new era of AI and onchain assets such as stablecoins and RWAs—domains where traditional smart contracts lack the flexibility and compliance required. By embedding programmable policies directly into execution, Newton provides the secure foundation needed to bring global assets onchain with trust and composability.

### Mission and Vision

Magic Newton Foundation’s mission is to steward Newton as a neutral, decentralized policy engine for programmable compliance. By combining cryptographic proofs, cross-chain operability, and integration of real-time offchain data, Newton transforms compliance from a fragmented afterthought into a composable layer institutions, issuers, and developers can build on with confidence.

### Ecosystem and Use Cases

Newton establishes a framework to bring the \$250T global investable asset market—and hundreds of trillions more in RWAs—onchain by enforcing policy at the point of execution. Core use cases include:

- **Stablecoin and RWA applications** with embedded compliance
- **Issuers and institutions** maintaining compliance and market integrity
- **Autonomous AI agents** that operate safely with policy guardrails
- **Defi vaults** running automated trading and yield strategies securely

### How Newton Works

- **Cross-Chain Expressive Policies:** Developer-friendly, user-friendly guardrails that work seamlessly across chains
- **Dynamic Offchain Data Integration**: Real-time market signals and compliance input directly into policies
- **Trust-Minimized with Guarantees**: Verifies every onchain action taken by any entity is within predefined policy
